Introduction

Semantic Types

This is an experimental project seeking to understand, and then - if possible - simplify some of the ways the user interacts with, describes and organizes information in a digital/programmable system.

At its core, the goal of this exercise is to:

  • observe a class of interactions that drive the information in a system,
  • taking it all apart and then
  • put it back together with a new understanding of what makes it tick (and stick).

In doing so I'm not looking to find any definitive solutions but rather interesting perspectives, and hope that eventually that extra bit of insight will help me improve the way I approach writing code.
